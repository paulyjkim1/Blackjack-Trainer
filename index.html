Welcome to Blackjack Trainer!

This trainer will simulate blackjack hands every time you press New Deal. The player can then pick an action based on their cards and the dealer's face up card (hit, stand, double, split). The player's action will be compared to the "statistically correct decision" based on basic strategy. Go ahead and click "The Book" button if you need help remembering!

Assume multiple deck blackjack, no insurance or even money options, surrendering is not allowed, and doubling after split is allowed.
